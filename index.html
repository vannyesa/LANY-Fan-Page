ABOUT

LANY

LANY is a Los Angeles based pop band known for its dreamy, emotional, and modern sound. Their music blends atmospheric melodies with heartfelt lyrics that resonate deeply with listeners. Through their songs, LANY often explores themes of love, loss, vulnerability, and the journey of life, capturing emotions that feel both personal and universal. This distinctive style has allowed LANY to build a strong connection with fans around the world, making their music relatable, comforting, and timeless
